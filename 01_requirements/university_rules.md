## Highest-Priority Rules (Binding)
- Project must be practical and problem-solving in nature, related to course of study, and include an artefact.
- Project must include development lifecycle evidence, including testing, and justification of tools/techniques.
- Module has two components: professionalism (25%) and project including artefact/project management (75%).
- Demonstration/viva is compulsory and marked as must-pass in project marking sheet context.
- Final report submission is via Canvas; final report should be submitted through Turnitin.
- Final report must include literature review, artefact design/development/testing, conclusions, and critical evaluation.
- Project management evidence (for example logbook, milestones, Gantt/progress tracking) is expected.
