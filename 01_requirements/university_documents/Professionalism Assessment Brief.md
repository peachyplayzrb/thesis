# Professionalism Assessment

Portfolio Assessment - 25% of the module.

**You must pass this part of the module to pass the module overall.**

## Assessment Brief

You should write a **Companion Report** (approximately **2000 words**) to your main project report discussing the professionalism aspects of your project, including:

- The social impact, beneficial or detrimental, your artefact and project may have.
- The ethical issues posed by your artefact and project.
- The legal implications of your artefact and project, for example GDPR, the Equality Act, copyright issues, and other UK and EU laws.
- The security aspects of your artefact and project, for example handling of personal data, encryption, and user wellbeing.

If one of the topics above does not apply directly to your project, you should explain why and discuss it in a more general fashion, for example how it could become relevant if the project were extended.

Each section should include:

- A short introduction.
- The aspects relevant to your project and why they are relevant, with supporting research.
- Relevant references showing that you understand the topic and its connection to your project.

## Sources of information

- Relevant lectures on the module.
- Research performed on your project plus any additional reading.
- Findings and experience from building and testing your artefact.
- The ethical approval process and recommendations.

## Submission

- Via Canvas.
- Formative submission for feedback: milestone 4.
- Summative marked submission: milestone 6, alongside the final project report.
