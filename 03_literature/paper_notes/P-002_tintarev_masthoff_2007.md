research_problem:
What purposes explanations serve in recommender systems and how explanation quality should be evaluated.

method_or_system_type:
Conceptual survey of explanation goals and interaction patterns.

key_findings:
- Explanations serve multiple aims (for example transparency, trust, effectiveness, persuasion, scrutability, efficiency, satisfaction).
- Accuracy metrics alone are insufficient to evaluate recommender system quality.
- Explanation design is coupled with how recommendations are produced and presented.

limitations:
- Early survey; does not cover recent deep model explanation methods.
- Not specific to music playlist generation architecture.

relevance_to_thesis:
High relevance for thesis evaluation criteria and for justifying controllability/scrutability requirements.

theme_mapping:
- explainable_recommenders
- transparency
- scrutability
- human_centered_evaluation

gap_implications:
Supports framing that transparent and controllable systems need user-centered evaluation dimensions beyond ranking performance.

design_implications:
- Include explanation objectives explicitly in architecture and evaluation plan.
- Preserve inspectable recommendation logic to support scrutability.
